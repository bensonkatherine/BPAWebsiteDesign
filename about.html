About Us

Stage Frights

Formed in late 2018 by childhood friends Hector Searight and Vlad Kohls, Stage Frights originally performed classical rock songs from bands such as Pink Floyd, Led Zepplin, and The Rolling Stones. As they grew more comfortable performing with eachother, Hector and Vlad began to branch out more, gravitating to the horrorcore rock style they are best known for today. After the release of their first single Heart of Darkness they added their third and final member in early 2020 NANSDF AFDIDIF, and released thier first EP, Into the Graveyard later that year. Since then the band has released sevral more singles, including their newest album, The Raven, and plan on releasing another album in the upcoming months.

Hector Seawright

Hector is the talented guitarist and co-founder of Stage Frights. Known for his electrifying riffs and theatrical performances, Hector brings a chilling energy to the band's signature dark sounds. Hector co-founded Stage Frights to merge his passion for rock music and love of all things horror, helping to fill a niche in the rock community. With his intense stage presence, Hector is instrumental in creating the iconic atmosphere that defines Stage Frights ' performances and keeps fans coming back for more

Vlad Kohls

When Vlad was approached by his childhood friend Hector to be the drummer in his new band Stage Frights,it was a no-brainier wether or not he would join. Vlad's drumming really shines through with the distinctive beat recognizable in all of Stage Frights' songs. Through his intense love for the drumset and creating music, Vlad has composed most of the original songs that Stage Frights perform. His skill and passion is palpable when Vlad is seen performing during concerts.

Ryan Clarke

While Ryan present during the original formation of the band, he has been just as instrumental in creating the atmosphere that Stage Frights is known for today. Through his commitment to performing with as much intensity as possible Ryan has made his mark with many memorable moments throughout his time as Stage Frights singer.
